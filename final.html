CSC 106 Final Project

This program is our final project for CSC 106. It is a game similar to asteroids called bitmoji asteroids. To play the game all you have to do is use the arrow keys to move the character you choose to avoid the asteroids. Each asteroid that crosses the screen is worth 50 points. When you hit 3000 points you will get to level 2 where the asteroids start to come faster from the right side. After you hit 10000 points they will come even faster from the top. If you get to 20000 points you will win the game. Try your best and see how long you can survive. Good luck. The video below shows an example of how the game works. I hope you enjoy our game.
